Kanyathon 2025 — Master Data & Student Sales Dashboards

Project Summary
Kanyathon 2025 is a social-impact event (marathons and games) focused on empowering and educating the girl child. This repository contains the master data work and sales-dashboard deliverables I developed as Chief Information Officer (CIO) to support event operations, reporting and ticket-sales performance tracking.

Key Responsibilities
-Managed registrations for 6,500+ participants.
-Led digital data initiatives to improve transparency and reporting.
-Coordinated volunteer allocation and monitored ticket sales targets.
-Data & Dashboard Work.
-Consolidated and cleaned raw data from multiple sources using Excel.
-Built Excel dashboards for:
i)Master Data: Registrations and metrics.
ii)Sales: Team-wise and section-wise ticket sales tracking.

Impact
-Improved reporting accuracy and operational efficiency.
-Enabled targeted follow-ups through sales performance insights.
-Supported data-backed decision-making during the event.

Data Collection & Cleaning | Excel | Dashboarding | Reporting & Analytics | CRM-style documentation | Process optimization | Data Management
